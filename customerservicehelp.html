How do I book a room with Hitaishi Rooms?

Visit our Booking page to check availability and reserve online, or call our support team at +91-123-456-7890.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48 hours prior to check-in are fully refundable. Partial refunds may apply for later cancellations. Contact us for details.

Do you offer group discounts?

Groups of 10 or more can avail discounts up to 20%. Email support@hitaishirooms.com for a quote.

Are pets allowed?

Pets are welcome in designated rooms. A pet fee applies. Inform us during booking.

How can I access Wi-Fi?

Wi-Fi credentials are provided at check-in. Contact the front desk or view our Wi-Fi guide.

What are your check-in/check-out times?

Check-in is at 2 PM, and check-out is at 11 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request.

Do you have accessible rooms?

Yes, we offer accessible rooms with features like roll-in showers. Contact us to confirm availability.

How do I join the Hitaishi Loyalty Program?

Sign up on our Loyalty page or contact our team to enroll and earn rewards.

Can I host events at Hitaishi Rooms?

Yes, we offer event spaces for weddings, conferences, and more. Visit our Events page for details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash at the property.
